An overview of participant selection process

Through the GPS and JUNO projects, we have partnered with >140 institutions globally. Application for this course was advertised to the wider GPS and JUNO networks.

The application window was from the 2nd of August - 16th of September 2022. The application form can be viewed below.

We prioritised the selection of applicants with the following characteristics:

  • from lower- and middle-income countries,
  • are early career researchers (i.e., either masterâ€™s students, PhD students, Postdocs or Research staff),

  • can demonstrate that this course would be of benefit to their current and future career aspirations.
